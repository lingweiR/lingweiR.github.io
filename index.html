Research Assistant to Professor James O’ Donovan (Full-time, Six Months)

City University of Hong Kong

Oct 2023 – Apr 2024 Hong Kong

Transaction Costs and Cost Mitigation in Option Investment Strategies

We investigate the impact of transaction costs on the strength of option return anomalies to examine whether the abnormal profits of these influential anomalies can be explained by arbitrage costs that deter potential arbitrage investors. We find that none of investigated anomalies survives the adjustment of transaction costs and look-ahead-bias ,which implies that option return anomalies can be attributed to the mispricing which can not be captured due to transaction costs . Therefore, it is essential to consider the impact of transaction costs in the investigation of implementable option investment universe.
